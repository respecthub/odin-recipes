Soy Sauce Eggs

Description

boiled eggs that are marinated in soy sauce for added flavor. Its a great way to get your protein in while adding a twisted to the standard boiled egg.

Ingredients

  • egg
  • soy sauce
  • rice wine
  • water

Steps

  1. Boil your eggs in a pot til your desired tenderness
  2. Peel your eggs and place them in a marinating bowl.
  3. Add soy sauce, rice wine, and water into the bowl and let this sit in the fridge for at least 6 hrs and up to a week
